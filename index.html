About the Game

In "Cookie Crush," players enter a sweet universe filled with various cookie-themed puzzles. This match-3 game offers hundreds of levels where you match delicious cookies to clear the board. Each level provides unique challenges designed to test your puzzle-solving skills, all set against a vibrant and colorful backdrop.

FAQ

  • How do I start playing?

    Just download the app and tap on ‘Play’.

  • Is the game free?

    Yes, it's free with optional in-app purchases.

  • Can I play offline?

    Yes, enjoy the game without an internet connection.

  • How do I get more lives?

    Lives refill over time, or you can purchase them.

  • Are there any multiplayer options?

    Currently, it's a single-player game.

  • How to use power-ups?

    Tap on them during gameplay to activate.

  • What are the stars for?

    Stars unlock further levels and rewards.

  • Can I replay previous levels?

    Yes, you can return and improve your scores.

  • What happens if I can’t complete a level?

    You can use power-ups or restart the level.

  • Customer support contact?

    Check the support section in the game settings.

How to Play

  • Match Cookies:

    Line up three or more identical cookies to clear them from the board.

  • Use Power-ups:

    Earn and strategically use power-ups to clear difficult obstacles.

  • Achieve Objectives:

    Complete the level’s specific objectives to progress.

  • Collect Stars:

    Earn stars based on your performance to unlock new levels.
